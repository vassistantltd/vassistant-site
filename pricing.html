Retainer Packages

A retainer package gives you a pre-paid, fixed amount of hours per month (minimum 3-month commitment), to be used as needed. This allows you to budget monthly, without any surprise costs! I'm happy to arrange a custom retainer – feel free to get in touch to discuss.

  • Time is tracked and rounded to the nearest 15 minutes
  • Hours do not carry over month-to-month
  • Briefing time is included within your package
