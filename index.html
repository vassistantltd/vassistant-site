Meet Your Assistant

Hello, and thank you for stopping by! I’m the heart and hands behind V Assistant Ltd — a virtual assistant service built for the dreamers, the doers, and the small business owners keeping the UK moving.

With a deep understanding of what it takes to run a business, our team brings experience from a wide range of industries — from building and construction, to vehicle servicing, waste disposal, beauty and personal care, and more.

We know the pressures you face. Endless paperwork, invoices, customer emails, missed appointments… It can become overwhelming. That’s where we step in. Our mission is simple: to take the tedious admin off your shoulders, and give you the time and mental space to focus on what truly matters — your clients, your craft, your growth, and your peace of mind.

Whether you're a one-person show or managing a tight team, I’m here to provide the support you didn’t know you needed — until now.
